135 feet long

60.7 feet tall

50 foot long neck


Longest Tail: Diplodocus carnegii


45 foot long tail


Smallest Adult Dinosaur: Compsognathus longipes


4 feet long

45-59 feet long


Second Largest Theropod: Carcharodontosaurus saharicus


45-59 feet long

3629 kilograms

54 feet long

49 feet long
